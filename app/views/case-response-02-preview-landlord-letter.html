Dear {{ globals.case_landlordname2 }},

We have received an application from your tenant requesting a market rent determination for the address above.

You can view and respond to their application online. A copy is also included with this message.

The tenant has not yet paid the application fee, so their application is currently on hold.

What you need to do

You have 28 days from the date of this message to respond to this application. You can respond online or by completing the MR2 reply form included with this message.

If you use the MR2 form, you must send your response to your tenant as well as to the Tribunal.

You do not have to respond, but if you do not, the Tribunal will make its decision based only on your tenant’s application.

What happens next

If the tenant does not pay the application fee by {{ globals.case_tenantfeeduedate }}, the case will be closed. We will let you know if this happens.

However, we recommend that you respond to the application now to avoid delays if the fee is paid.

  1. If you respond online or through the the MR2 form, your tenant will have 14 days to respond online or through the MR3 tenant reply form.
  2. The Tribunal will make its decision based on the tenant’s application and any replies received.
  3. When the Tribunal has made its decision, we will contact you by post and email.

If we need anything else from you, we will contact you.

Yours sincerely,
